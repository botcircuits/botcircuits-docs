Botcircuits is an AI SaaS platform designed to provide the fastest and easiest way to develop LLM-native Conversational AI Agents, providing you with the best reliability and control over conversational flow for real-world applications. 

## Why BotCircuits?

BotCircuits offers an intuitive way to design and manage conversation flows, ensuring that business logic and workflows are followed precisely, rather than relying on LLM prompts, which are limited and primarily suited for knowledge retrieval tasks. 

With BotCircuits, your AI agents not only communicate effectively but also deliver **reliable** and **controlled** responses perfectly aligned with your real-world business requirements.

## Key Features

- **Conversation Understanding and Response**: End-users will experience human-like conversations with AI agents that are capable of understanding natural conversation patterns and responding dynamically in personalized ways.

- **No-Code Conversation Flow Editor**: Design custom conversation flows effortlessly using our drag-and-drop web interface. Seamlessly connect to endpoints like APIs, code, databases, and more.

- **Single Agent Publishing on Multiple Channels**: Build a single agent and publish it across text, real-time voice, and popular omnichannel platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, and more.

- **Easily integrate frequently used tools**: Simply integrate frequently used tools like Google Sheet, Meet, Shopify, etc., into your AI agents to extend their capabilities.
