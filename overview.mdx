BotCircuits is a conversational AI SaaS platform that enables small and medium-sized businesses to create and deploy AI-powered voice and text-based agents within minutes. 
## Why BotCircuits?

- **No-Code UI Editor**: Create voice and text-based AI agents using our intuitive drag-and-drop interface. No coding skills required.
- **Omnichannel Support**: Seamlessly integrate your AI agents with popular messaging platforms like WhatsApp, Slack, and more.
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate workflows and eliminate low-value tasks so your teams can focus on what matters most.
- **Latest LLMs**: Pair the best LLMs with your knowledge base and prepare your agents that deliver the best or bring your own to power your conversational agents.

## Key Features

- **Voice & Text Integration**: Build AI agents that work across multiple channels, including phone, SMS, WhatsApp, and more.
- **AI-Powered Conversations**: Enable natural, human-like conversations with AI-driven responses.
- **Custom Workflows**: Create customized workflows for different use cases like customer support, lead generation, and booking management.
